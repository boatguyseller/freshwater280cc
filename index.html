1997 Sea Ray 280 Cuddy Cabin

This freshwater-only Sea Ray 280 Cuddy Cabin has been owned by the same family since 1999 and has always been stored indoors. In recent years, the boat has been kept in a privately owned heated storage suite and has been carefully maintained locally throughout its ownership.

Powered by a MerCruiser 7.4L engine, this boat offers a versatile platform for cruising, day boating, entertaining, or fishing on Lake Erie. The spacious cockpit provides comfortable seating for family and friends, while the cuddy cabin offers shelter and convenience for extended days on the water.

The combination of the powerful 7.4L MerCruiser, spacious cockpit, cuddy cabin, and included tri-axle trailer makes this an excellent Lake Erie boat. It is equally at home cruising the islands, spending the day at anchor, entertaining family and friends, or serving as a versatile platform for fishing.

The boat includes an Eagle tri-axle trailer, front and rear bimini tops for full cockpit shade, a complete camper enclosure, and two mooring covers (one is a backup). Whether you're looking for a comfortable day cruiser or a boat that can easily be adapted for fishing, this Sea Ray offers exceptional versatility and value.

Known items that could benefit from attention include the AM/FM stereo, which is a great opportunity to upgrade to a modern Bluetooth unit, and a detached speedometer needle. The speedometer itself may still function, but the needle is no longer attached.
